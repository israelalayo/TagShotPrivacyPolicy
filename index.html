Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

Our app (“CameraPo”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Information Collection

CameraPo may collect basic photo metadata, including the date, time, and location of capture.

Purpose: This information is used solely to improve the app functionality and provide context for your photos.

Retention: Metadata is automatically deleted after 90 days.

Storage: All data is stored locally on your device. No metadata is uploaded to our servers.

2. User Accounts

CameraPo does not require you to create an account and does not collect usernames, passwords, or other login credentials.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

4. User Control and Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via email. Uninstalling the app also permanently removes all locally stored data.

Metadata is automatically deleted after 90 days, even if no action is taken by the user.

5. External Links

The app may include links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external services.

6. Children’s Privacy

CameraPo is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected in this document with a new "Last updated" date.
